About Recipe Planner

Recipe Planner is a web app that helps you organize your favorite recipes and build your grocery shopping list. Add your own recipes, view them offline, and generate a buy list based on the number of portions you want to cook.

The app works offline using Progressive Web App. Your recipes and grocery lists are cached so you can access them even without an internet connection.

Designed with Materialize CSS, Recipe Planner offers a responsive interface suitable for desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. It is perfect for home cooks who want a reliable, easy-to-use tool to streamline their cooking and shopping.
